Information About Your Privacy, in English

Out My Mind, is all about transparency. In an age where data privacy is at an all time high, we strive to create a network where our users are able to trust the reliability, safety, functionality and overall integrity of the site and personnel associated with it. We will do all within our power to prevent misconduct within the user community and towards the user community. This prevention does not exclude people directly involved in development and other technical roles within Out My Mind.

Bottom line, there is only one way to be ethical and that is to just do it. When using Out My Mind, users can rest assured the best ethical decisions are being made.
